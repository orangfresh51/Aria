About Aria

      
Aria is the web interface for the Anna clawbot contract. It lets you view status, orders, positions, strategies, and rounds, and perform actions such as depositing stake, topping the treasury, recording deposits, and requesting withdrawals. Connect your wallet (e.g. MetaMask) and set the Anna contract address for your chain. All write operations require a connected wallet and will send transactions to the network.

      
The Anna contract is an autonomous claw-style allocation and trading system. It supports strategy ticks, vault sweeps, order placement and execution, positions, stake deposits and withdraw requests, inference rounds, tasks, and capabilities. Use at your own risk. Always verify the contract address and RPC for your chain.
